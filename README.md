## Getting Started

### Step 1: Install MetaMask
- Download and install MetaMask from [metamask.io](https://metamask.io/)
- Create a new wallet or import an existing one
- Make sure to save your seed phrase securely

### Step 2: Add Base Sepolia Network
1. Open MetaMask and click on the network dropdown (top center)
2. Click "Add Network" or "Custom RPC"
3. Fill in these details:
   - **Network Name**: Base Sepolia
   - **RPC URL**: `https://sepolia.base.org`
   - **Chain ID**: `84532`
   - **Currency Symbol**: `ETH`
   - **Block Explorer**: `https://sepolia-explorer.base.org`
4. Click "Save"

### Step 3: Get Test ETH
1. Visit [Base Sepolia Faucet](https://www.alchemy.com/faucets/base-sepolia)
2. Connect your MetaMask wallet
3. Request test ETH (you'll need this for transactions)
4. Wait for the ETH to appear in your wallet

### Step 4: Connect to Platform
1. Visit [https://crowdfundweb.vercel.app/](https://crowdfundweb.vercel.app/)
2. Click "Connect Wallet" button
3. Approve the connection in MetaMask
4. If prompted, switch to Base Sepolia network

## Using the Platform

### Creating a Campaign
1. Click "Create Campaign" button
2. Fill out the form:
   - **Title**: Name of your campaign
   - **Description**: What you're raising funds for
   - **Goal Amount**: How much ETH you need
   - **Deadline**: When the campaign ends
   - **Image**: Upload a campaign image (stored on IPFS)
   - **Category**: Type of campaign
   - **Creator Info**: Your name or organization
3. Click "Create Campaign"
4. Confirm the transaction in MetaMask
5. Wait for confirmation

**Note**: Campaign images are stored on IPFS (decentralized storage) for permanent availability.

### Donating to Campaigns
1. Browse campaigns on the main page
2. Click on any campaign card to see details
3. Enter the amount you want to donate
4. Click "Donate Now"
5. Confirm the transaction in MetaMask

### Managing Your Activities
Navigate using the tabs at the top:
- **Home**: Browse all available campaigns and platform overview
- **My Campaigns**: View and manage campaigns you've created
- **My Donations**: See your complete donation history with transaction details
- **My Withdrawals**: Track your withdrawal records and transaction history
- **Analytics**: View platform statistics and insights
- **FAQ**: Get answers to frequently asked questions

### Withdrawing Funds (Campaign Creators)
**Important**: You can only withdraw funds after your campaign reaches its goal and the deadline passes.

1. Go to "My Campaigns" tab
2. Find your successful campaign (goal reached)
3. **Upload Proof of Fund Utilization**:
   - Click on your campaign to view details
   - Upload documents showing how you plan to use/used the funds
   - Proof documents are permanently stored on IPFS
4. Once proof is uploaded, return to "My Campaigns"
5. Click "Withdraw Funds" button (now available)
6. Confirm the transaction in MetaMask
7. Track your withdrawal in "My Withdrawals" tab

**Note**: Proof of fund utilization is mandatory before withdrawal. This ensures transparency and builds trust with donors.

## Troubleshooting

### "Connect Your Wallet" Message
- Make sure MetaMask is installed and unlocked
- Click "Connect Wallet" and approve the connection
- Ensure you're on the Base Sepolia network

### Transaction Errors
- **Insufficient funds**: Get more test ETH from the faucet
- **Wrong network**: Switch to Base Sepolia in MetaMask
- **Transaction failed**: Try again with higher gas limit

### MetaMask Issues
- **Not connecting**: Refresh the page and try again
- **Wrong network**: Manually switch to Base Sepolia
- **No test ETH**: Visit the faucet link above

### Campaign Not Loading
- Check your internet connection
- Make sure you're on Base Sepolia network
- Refresh the page

## Need More Help?

If you're still having issues:
1. Make sure MetaMask is properly installed and set up
2. Verify you're on the Base Sepolia network
3. Ensure you have test ETH for transactions
4. Try refreshing the page or restarting your browser
